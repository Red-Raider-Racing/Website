About Us

Red Raider Racing is a student-led organization that gives Texas Tech University students the opportunity to get hands-on experience by designing, fabricating, and marketing a Formula SAE car. Each year, our team designs and manufactures a brand-new FSAE vehicle to compete in the International Collegiate Design Competition. Our student organization is composed of 14 officers as well as over 100+ members that work together to develop a technically sound and competitive car.

Experience

FSAE gives you a head-start on your career by providing real-world engineering experiences that engineering companies look for.

Networking

A number of events, info sessions, and competitions provide members with opportunities to network with professionals in the automotive industry. These networking opportunites can learn to internships, job offers, and team sponsorships.

Community

FSAE members are united by a sense of camaraderie, passion, and dedication. You will meet many friends, and mentors throughout your time in Red Raider Racing and become part of a supportive, tight-knit community.
